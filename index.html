About us

Lindesberg Equestrian Center is a small tightknit community of riders. Who focus on learning more about all disciplines of riding and horsemanship.
Located about 2 hours drive west of Stockholm in the idyllic region of Bergslagen. With it's lush forrests, steep hills and mountains, plenty of rivers and lakes it's an ideal place for trekking with horses.
As one of our members you gain access to: one outdoor paddock and two indoor riding arenas, one was finished in 2018 and is heated during winter. All three arenas are of international competition standard in both measurement and quality.
You will also be able to rent our horses for lessons, competitions or just a peacefull trekk on one of the dozen trails that surround Lindesberg Equestrian Center.
On staff we have two trainers, Christian who specializes in showjumping and Emma who specializes in dressage, and two stablehands, Maria and Tommy, who cares for our horses and the facility.
